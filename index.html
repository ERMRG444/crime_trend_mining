The Challenge

Rising Crime Rates

Urban sprawl and socioeconomic challenges have led to an increase in complex, organized criminal activities that overwhelm traditional policing.

Siloed Data Systems

Crucial intelligence is often trapped in fragmented databases across different government departments, preventing proactive measures.

The Dark Figure of Crime

A massive percentage of crimes remain underreported. Lack of citizen engagement platforms makes mapping real crime density difficult.

Data-Driven Solutions

Spatial & Temporal Analysis

Interactive heatmaps that identify crime hotspots based on location and time, enabling optimized patrol deployment and resource allocation.

Predictive AI Models

Utilizing scikit-learn and historical datasets to forecast potential criminal activities, shifting from reactive responses to preventative strategies.

Citizen Reporting Portal

Empowering the public with an easy-to-use mobile-responsive system to report incidents securely, reducing the dark figure of crime.

Open Portal

Unified Data Integration

A robust backend processing pipeline to ingest, clean, and catalog data from multiple agencies into one centralized real-time dashboard.

SDG 16: Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions

Our Crime Trend Mining solution directly aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 16. By utilizing transparent predictive models and integrating citizen feedback, we aim to significantly reduce all forms of violence, promote the rule of law, and build effective, accountable, and inclusive law enforcement institutions at all levels.
